Blitz 1830:
A Western Top-Down Shooter Game

Controls

Use the left and right mouse buttons to fire your pistols. Use the WASD keys to move your character. Take cover and use tactical reloading to make every shot count!

About

Blitz 1830 is a top-down shooter game that emphasizes strategic gameplay and precision shooting mechanics. Take on waves of enemies and master the dual-wielding mechanics to become the ultimate gunslinger!
